Help

How old should I be in order to use the website?

In compliance with UK (and general EU) laws, one must be at least 18 years old in order to register and make use of the cocktails recipes.

Am I required to provide proof of my age?

We trust you. Of course nobody lies on the Internet.

Do I need to be registered in order to upload my cocktail recipes?

Yes. An account is required in order to access the upload recipes page.

Can I rate my favourite cocktails?

You can use our sophisticated rating system in order to express your opinion on your favourite and least favourite cocktails.

Who are you?

We are students at the University of Glasgow. You can read more about us at the about page.
